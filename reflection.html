Reflection

To me road to hire was like the image above, a nontraditional path that leads to the same destination. This alternative path is more valuable & it also gets me there faster as long as I put in the work. My greatest strengths on the tech side is HTML & CSS. My greatest strengths on the proffesional development side is communication & problem solving skills. In my future role, I plan to capitalize my strengths by stressing the importance of communication in the work place & adding another great problem solver to their team will only elevate the team. I think my biggest areas of opportunity are perfectionism & time-management. I plan to apply a new ideology "First things come first" I will start classifying assignments by urgency & importance. Sometimes I find myself working on a different project when I have an assignment that I havent touched due in a couple hours so I plan to start managing the way I work on assignments. Overall, the Road To Hire experience has molded me into the individual I am today & i'm thankful for that.
